Translation notes

Key wording where the English needs nuance.

Seelenkrieg

Not merely “soul war.” In context, it carries psychological war, spiritual pressure, trauma, and endurance.

Mana

The song frames power as elemental life-force. The warning matters: hoarded power becomes destructive instead of healing.

Weißes Licht

In “Supernova,” white light is not only purity. It is salvation, obsession, and danger at the edge of annihilation.

Schwarzer Mann

In “Angst,” translate this as the bogeyman or dark fear figure from German childhood lore. The song is about manufactured fear and obedience, not race.
